Preliminary Images#

WRONG: 2X RMS NOISE. The following images were cleaned non-interactively using a circular mask of radius 2 arcsec centered on 04:55:45.854900 +30.33.03.73320 (J2000), the hogbom deconvolver with a Briggs robust weighting and no uv-taper, a cell size of 0.04 arcsec and 2000 cells, and cleaned down to a threhold of 1x the rms noise measured in the dirty image within an annulus (inner radius 4 arcsec; outer radius 10 arcsec). Things to improve: The cell size should be dependent on the robust parameter (cell size should sample the beam ~5-10 times and the beam depends on the robust parameter); the beam minor axis is borderline-sampled in the robust=0 image. We could also try uv-tapering. It is strange to clean down to 1x the rms noise measured in the dirty image, but I felt the residuals after cleaning to 4x or 3x were still quite large (i.e. I felt the residuals were still well above the noise and more signal could be extracted).
